Pakistan Traffic Signs Learning App

Overview

PakSigns is a dedicated educational helper tool designed to assist driving license candidates in learning Pakistani traffic signs, understanding road marking rules, and successfully passing their computerized driving license theory tests (DLIMS).

Features Included

  • Study Guides: Categorized directory of signs with Urdu and English translations.
  • Audio Pronunciation: High quality local text-to-speech option to read sign meanings out loud.
  • Interactive Road: Graphical visualizer displaying lane marking requirements.
  • DLIMS Mock Exam: Simulated mock tests under practice configurations and strict examination rules.

Usage Guidelines

Navigate between screens using the top application tabs. Customize your exam length and layout parameters under the "Test" setup configurations. Save complex signs with the star icon to review them in the "Saved" category.
